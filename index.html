History of Systems Engineering

Systems Engineering is the application of scientific and engineering efforts to transform an operation need into a description of performance parameters of the system, their configuration through the use of an iterative process of definition, synthesis, analysis, design, testing and evaluation. It also integrates technical parameters to ensure the compatibility of all program and functional interfaces in a way that optimizes the definition and design of the total system.
